Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about VeDrive, your go-to platform for driver distraction detection.

What is VeDrive?

VeDrive is a comprehensive driver distraction detection platform that utilizes advanced image, video, and live detection technologies to ensure safer driving. It monitors and alerts drivers about potential distractions such as drowsiness, yawning, and lack of attention.

How does VeDrive detect driver distraction?

VeDrive employs state-of-the-art algorithms using OpenCV and Mediapipe to analyze facial landmarks. By calculating metrics like eye aspect ratio, mouth aspect ratio, and head pose, it can accurately detect signs of drowsiness, yawning, and distraction in real-time.

Can VeDrive be used for both image and video analysis?

Yes, VeDrive supports image, video, and live feed analysis. You can upload images and videos for distraction detection, or use the live detection feature for real-time monitoring and alerts.

What kind of alerts does VeDrive provide?

VeDrive provides audio alerts through speech synthesis using pyttsx3. These alerts inform drivers about detected drowsiness, yawning, or distractions, helping them stay attentive and safe on the road.

How can I get started with VeDrive?

Getting started with VeDrive is simple. Visit our website, create an account, and choose the detection mode that suits your needs—whether it's for images, videos, or live feeds. Follow the on-screen instructions to upload your files or start live detection.
